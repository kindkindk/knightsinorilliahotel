Attractions in Orillia

Casino Rama
Looking to score big? Casino Rama is only a short drive away, and the odds are on big fun! Whether it's tossing the dice, a cool hand of poker or just a meal and a show, Casino Rama guarantees the time of your life.
Phone: 705-329-3325
Address: 5899 Rama Road, Rama, Ontar

Leacock Museum
The once summer home of Stephen Leacock, one of Canada's best known writers, is now home to a flurry of activities for all ages. Take the kids to the Discovery Place while enjoying yourself at the Shoreline CafÃ© overlooking beautiful Lake Couchiching. Be sure to stop by the book and gift shops.
Phone: 705-329-1908
Address: 50 Museum Drive, Orillia, Ontario

Mnjikaning Fish Weirs
Take a trip back in time to discover how the First Nations people guided and caught fish for thousands of years at this historical site.
Phone: 705-750-4936

OPP Museum
Located within the Ontario Provincial Police General Headquarters, the museum features a wide variety of exhibits, from clothing to cars. Feel free to take your time in browsing, as the museum is designed for self-guided tours. Souvenirs are available at the gift shop.
Phone: 705-329-6280
Address: 777 Memorial Ave, Orillia, Ontario

Orillia Museum of Art and History
If you've got the time, look for the clock tower in the heart of downtown Orillia. Within this building the finest art created in the area is on display; you can even create your own! Expand your palette by absorbing some of Orillia's culture and history.
Phone: 705-326-2159
Address: 30 Peter Street South, Orillia, Ontario

Orillia Opera House
The Orillia Opera House is located in the heart of Orillia, and is easily one of the most recognizable buildings in the city. Be sure to take in at least one of the many events hosted here, you won't regret it.
Phone: 705-325-2153
Address: 20 Mississaga Street West, Orillia, Ontario

Orillia Boat Cruises
Spend a pleasant day out on the water on Orillia's finest cruise ship: The Island Princess. Enjoy a meal, or learn the history of the land surrounding Lake Couchiching during a guided tour. Seats a 230 passengers, and is available for special excursions.
Phone: 705-325-2628

Sunshine and Company
Sunshine and Company is a tireless theatre group that work hard to entertain at the Opera House. They will have you feeling the joys of life, and the arts, that everyone should experience.
Phone: 705-325-2153
Address: 20 Mississaga Street West, Orillia, Ontario

Washago Cruise
Enjoy the scenery as you cruise the Severn River on a comfortable 12 passenger pontoon boat. This leisurely cruise will have you passing through the Green and Black Rivers, and seeing the first Hydro Electric power dam in Ontario. Three departures daily, reserve a spot today.
Phone: 705-689-0066

Orillia Farmers' Market
Take a walk downtown on a Saturday morning and you're sure to be greeted by smiling faces, the smell of freshly baked goods and meats. The Farmers' Market is open for business from 7:30 to 12:30, featuring food, friends and family events; you don't want to miss it.
Address: 20 Mississaga Street West, Orillia, Ontario.

Port Of Orillia
Orillia's Waterfront is the pride of the community and jewel of the Trent-Severn Waterway. Services include 222 fully serviced slips accommodating boats up to 80 feet, fully trained dock staff and a fishing pier, special events throughout the year and it's only steps from downtown. Reservations are accepted.
Phone: 705-326-6314
